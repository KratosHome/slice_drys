Dried Plum Jerky is a unique combination of rich fruity flavor and delicate texture. This jerky contains no added sugar, preservatives, or artificial colors, making it an ideal choice for those who value healthy eating.

100% natural ingredients – free from artificial additives and sweeteners.
Rich in nutrients – contains vitamins A, C, K, iron, and antioxidants.
Perfect snack – great for travel, work, or sports.
Refined taste – a sweet-tart flavor with a light fruity note.
