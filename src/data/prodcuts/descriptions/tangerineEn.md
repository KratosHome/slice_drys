Our dried mandarin jerky is a unique product that combines the classic taste of high-quality beef with an exotic hint of fresh mandarin. Made using traditional methods, it guarantees natural ingredients and a distinctive flavor in every bite. First, only the freshest meat is selected, free from artificial additives and preservatives. After marination, the meat is dried at low temperatures to preserve all the beneficial nutrients and natural taste, while also extending its shelf life without the need for artificial preservatives.
